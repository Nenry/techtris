How to play
  • Use the tile pieces to fill up a row.
  • Rows that fill up will clear and points will be rewarded.
  • Score multipliers added for multiple rows cleared at once
Instructions
  • Press P to Start/Pause/Unpause the game.
  • Press Up ⇧ to rotate piece
  • Press ⇦ to move left or ⇨ to move right
  • Press SPACE to do fast drop
  • Press R to Restart the game
